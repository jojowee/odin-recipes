Three Cup Tofu

Description

Crispy tofu with a savory ginger and garlic glaze.

Ingredients

  • Tofu - 1kg
  • Ginger - 20g
  • Shallot - 3pc
  • Garlic - 6 cloves
  • Soy Sauce - 100ml
  • Sesame Oil - 50ml
  • Rice Wine - 50ml
  • Red Chili Pepper - 2pc

Steps

  1. Mince the ginger and garlic. Cut the shallot and red chili into slices.

    Cut the tofu into cubes

  2. Add enough oil for frying into pot and turn to medium heat. When hot, add tofu and fry until they are golden and crispy outside, flipping to fry all sides.

    Take the tofu out and set aside.

  3. Heat up a wok over medium-high heat. Add some oil and the ginger, garlic, and shallots. Stir for about 30 seconds.

    Add tofu, soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice wine.

  4. Mix and let it cook for a few minutes until most of the sauce is gone. Add red chili and stir for another minute.
